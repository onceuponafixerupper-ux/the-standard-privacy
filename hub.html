📝 Reply Templates

The Empathy Open
I hear you. I was exactly where you are — starting over for the hundredth time and wondering if this time would be any different...
🎯
The Three Things
Can I share something that changed everything for me? I stopped trying to be perfect. Now I just hold three things: protein, water, movement...
🔥
The Reframe
You didn't fail. Your plan failed. There's a massive difference. If your system needs a perfect day to work, it was never going to last...
🤝
The Soft CTA
If you want, I've been using an app that makes this stupid simple. You just photograph your plate and it does the macro math. No calorie obsessing...
🌅
The Morning Check-in
Day [X] of holding the standard. Three things: protein, water, movement. That's it. No perfect days required. Who's holding it with me today?
📸
The App Demo Reply
Ok I have to share this because it literally changed my tracking game. I photograph my plate, the AI reads my macros, I adjust if needed. Done in 10 seconds...
❤️
The Validation
Hey — the fact that you're here asking this question means you haven't given up. That matters more than you think. Most people quit quietly...
⚡ Sarge Voice Rules
DO: Lead with empathy. Share personal experience. Offer one specific tip. Mention the app only if it naturally fits.
DON'T: Sound like a sales pitch. Use "soldier" or military bro language. Shame anyone. Promise specific results.
TONE: Like a friend who's been there, got through it, and is reaching back to help you up. Direct but warm. "You can do this. I believe that."
